October 2015
<-September2015 November 2015->
October started with an away game at Norwich, where Leicester won 2-1, thanks to goals from defender, Jeff Schlupp and striker, Jamie Vardy. Southampton away next, and like the Stoke and Aston Villa games, Leicester 2-0 down. But two goals from Jamie Vardy gained Leicester a well-deserved point.

Manager Claudio Ranieri promised to treat his squad with pizza, if they kept a clean sheet in one of Leicester’s games. Leicester’s next game was against Crystal Palace, where Leicester won 1-0, with another goal from Jamie Vardy. Pizza secured for the squad.

This was Vardy's 7th consecutive game where he scored, and the media was questioning whether Vardy could break Van Nistelrooy’s goalscoring record of scoring in 10 consecutive games.

On Halloween, Leicester faced West Brom at The Hawthorns. West Brom took the lead, and two goals from Riyad Mahrez and a goal from Jamie Vardy, gave Leicester a 3-1 lead. West Brom did score a penalty with six minutes to go, but Leicester held on to win 3-2 win. The end of the month left Leicester 3rd, below leaders Manchester City and second placed Arsenal.

My Opinions: Great comeback against Southampton and a good run after wins against Palace and West Brom.

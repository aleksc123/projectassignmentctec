September 2015
<-August2015 October 2015->
September began with a home match against Aston Villa. Leicester won the game 3-2 after coming back from 2-0 down, with goals from defender Ritchie De Laet, Jamie Vardy and new signing, Nathan Dyer. All three goals scored by the foxes were scored within 17 minutes. An incredible comeback.

Stoke next, and again, Leicester 2-0 down. Goals from Jamie Vardy and Riyad Mahrez for Leicester and the match ended 2-2. The final game of September was a 2-5 defeat against Arsenal.

The end of the month left Leicester 7th in the league with 12 points.

My Opinions: Good comebacks from the games against Aston Villa and Stoke, disappointing loss against Arsenal, where Leicester played well in the first half of the match.
